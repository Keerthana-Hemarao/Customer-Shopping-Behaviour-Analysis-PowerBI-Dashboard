# Customer Behaviour Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover key insights into purchasing patterns, subscription impact, and revenue distribution. The dashboard is designed for business stakeholders to quickly understand customer trends and make informed decisions.

---

## Business Objective
The objective of this analysis is to:
- Understand customer demographics and buying behavior
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Evaluate the impact of subscriptions, promotions, and shipping preferences
- Support data-driven strategies for revenue growth and customer retention

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Customers
- Average Purchase Amount
- Average Review Rating
- Subscription vs Non-Subscription Customers

These metrics provide a high-level snapshot of customer engagement and business performance.

---

## Key Insights

### Customer Segmentation
- Middle-aged and Adult customers contribute the highest share of revenue
- Young Adults show higher purchase frequency but lower average spend
- Senior customers maintain stable purchasing behavior

### Subscription Behavior
- Majority of customers are non-subscribers
- Subscribed customers demonstrate higher repeat purchase potential
- Subscription adoption presents a clear growth opportunity

### Product & Category Performance
- Clothing is the top-performing category in both revenue and sales volume
- Accessories and Footwear are strong secondary contributors
- Outerwear shows lower demand, indicating seasonal or niche behavior

### Revenue & Sales Trends
- Higher sales volume does not always translate to higher revenue
- Repeat purchases drive revenue more than one-time transactions
- Promotions influence purchase frequency but must be optimized for margin control

### Shipping Preferences
- Express and Free Shipping are the most preferred options
- Faster shipping correlates with higher customer satisfaction

---

## Business Recommendations
- Promote subscription plans with targeted incentives
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories
- Personalize promotions based on customer purchase frequency
- Optimize shipping strategies to balance cost and experience
